## Product: "Next Generation Legal Service AI Agent System"

### Core Products (Four Components)

1. **WiseChat** - AI Legal Assistant
   - Integrates legal knowledge base from 125+ countries
   - Handles complex cross-border compliance consulting
   - Recommends collaborating lawyers for complex issues
   - Acts as a "super legal assistant" with high-precision logical reasoning

2. **WiseTools** - Contract Review System
   - Efficient contract review functionality
   - Embodies senior lawyer review experience into system capabilities
   - Highlights high-risk clauses with priority and provides solutions
   - **Claimed efficiency gain:** 3-5x productivity increase
   - **Time reduction:** 4-hour review → 5 minutes

3. **WiseSearch** - Legal Data Retrieval
   - One-stop intelligent search for HKEX prospectuses, announcements, Hong Kong legislation
   - Natural language query understanding
   - **Time reduction:** 2-day search → 20 minutes
   - Precision: Top 5 results contain required answers

4. **WiseCourse** - AI Legal Education
   - "AI Tutor" system for personalized learning
   - Currently used at PolyU and City University of Hong Kong
   - Guides proper AI usage habits
   - Helps teachers establish scientific educational feedback mechanisms

### Positioning
- Described as a "Legal Co-worker" (digital employee living in your computer)
- Targets: Large, medium, and small law firms; enterprises going global; corporate legal teams
- **Philosophy:** "Not to replace lawyers, but to empower them" (解放律師，讓法律人把時間花在法律上)
- **Value proposition:** "Democratization of capability" - enabling small/medium firms to provide high-end services
